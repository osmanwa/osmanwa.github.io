About

I work at the intersection of biology, data science, and artificial intelligence, helping biotech and pharmaceutical companies accelerate their research through computational approaches.

My work involves developing and applying computational methods to analyze complex biological datasets, from genomics and proteomics to high-throughput screening data. I build pipelines, design algorithms, and create tools that enable researchers to extract meaningful insights from their data.

With a deep understanding of both the biological domain and modern computational techniques, I bridge the gap between experimental biology and data science, ensuring that computational solutions are both scientifically rigorous and practically applicable.

I'm passionate about open science, reproducible research, and contributing to the computational biology community through publications, open-source tools, and collaborative projects.

Areas of Expertise

Bioinformatics

Sequence analysis, genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics. Building pipelines for NGS data processing, variant calling, and differential expression analysis.

Machine Learning & AI

Developing predictive models for drug discovery, biomarker identification, and biological pattern recognition. Deep learning for image analysis and sequence modeling.

Computational Biology

Systems biology, pathway analysis, network biology, and structural bioinformatics. Integrating multi-omics data to understand biological mechanisms.

Data Infrastructure

Designing scalable data processing systems, cloud-based analytics platforms, and reproducible research workflows. Containerization and workflow management for large-scale analyses.

Software Development

Building robust, well-documented tools and libraries for the computational biology community. Python, R, and modern software engineering practices for scientific computing.

Research Collaboration

Working closely with experimental biologists, clinicians, and other domain experts to translate research questions into computational solutions and actionable insights.
